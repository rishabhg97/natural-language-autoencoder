AV & AR internals

Same recipe for both models: the AV is the full parent with an injection hook; the AR is the parent truncated to the boundary, final LayerNorm swapped for Identity, plus a linear value head.

RL training loop

After AV-SFT and AR-SFT, both are refined together: the AV is a GRPO actor; the AR is the live reward model / critic. The reward is just the reconstruction quality of what the AV said.
