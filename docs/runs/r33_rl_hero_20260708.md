## Retry Lineage

| Run | Terminal state | Evidence retained | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|
| Original 342-update hero | Guard-stopped at update `63` after two large KL excursions | Logs only; no checkpoint | Require three consecutive KL exceedances and keep other actor guards |
| Guard retry 1 | Guard-stopped during rollout `26` by a relative p95-increase rule at only `170.55` tokens | Logs only; no checkpoint | Replace relative length trend with absolute telemetry/guards |
| Length-cap retry 2 | Guard-stopped during rollout `253` after p95 `233.85`, then `232.70`, despite zero truncation | Model checkpoint `iter_0000228` | Remove p95 as an abort condition; retain truncation/parser guards |
| Resume-228 retry 3 | Complete through update `342`; both post-evals passed | Historical `iter_0000342` | Retain as the internal milestone; publication effect later invalidated |

Retry 2 used `--no-save-optim`. Retry 3 therefore restored the update-228
model, RNG, rollout counter, and `global_dataset_state_dict_227.pt`, but
started fresh Adam state at the same constant LR. Miles resumed from rollout
`228` and continued to the absolute endpoint `342`. The selected model lineage
contains 342 updates; retry-2 work after checkpoint 228 was discarded when the
continuation loaded that checkpoint.
